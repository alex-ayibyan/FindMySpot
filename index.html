The Parking Problem

In busy urban areas like Brussels, Antwerp and Ghent, people experience daily problems finding parking spaces:

Time Loss

Users waste significant time searching for parking spots, causing frustration and unnecessary delays.

Increased Traffic

Constant circling for parking increases traffic congestion and unnecessary emissions.

Uncertainty & Frustration

Users often can't know if a spot is actually available, leading to stress and wasted effort.

Our Smart Solution

1

Real-Time Parking Information

Our app uses crowdsourced data from users to collect information about available parking spots. When a user leaves a parking spot, they can report it directly in the app with a simple tap.

2

GPS-Based Location

The app uses GPS to determine the user's location and shows the nearest available parking spots based on real-time data shared by other users, presented on a map with clear availability markers.

3

In-App Payments

Users can pay for parking directly through the app without needing physical tickets or coins. The app also tracks parking duration and alerts users when their time is almost up.

4

User Feedback System

Our feedback system allows users to rate the accuracy of shared parking information. Active users who provide accurate information are rewarded with points or badges.
